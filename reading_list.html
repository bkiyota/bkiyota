Reading Log

This page features a timeline for the books that I have read since 2015. I used to avoid books like the plague, likely due to my disinterest in the books chosen for high school assignments. My feelings really changed when I had the chance to read a few books on my own schedule, talking about things that I find interesting. Now reading is one of my favourite activities to chill and relax, and my list of books to read continues to grow at a much faster pace than I can actually read them! I enjoy non-fiction science books for the most part, but have been slowly expanding the genres I've looked at.

Note that the dates listed on the timeline are rough approximations. The backgrounds for each slide are also coloured according to the categorical genre that I've arbitrarily chosen.
